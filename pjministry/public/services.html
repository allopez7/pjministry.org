PJ Ministry Supplies

All provisions that were supplied to the homeless from PJ Ministry are brand new because we believe we are giving our best to the Lord. Coats, clothes, shoes, socks, blankets, back packs, sleeping bags, water bottles, toiletries, medical supplies, radios, batteries, gift certificates, bus passes, gasoline, breaks for the van, water pump, registration and licensing, 4 wheel carts, bibles and books, laundry supplies & quarters, lots of food. Other material donations that have been made are also a vital part of the blessings we receive. As our people begin to get their housing we continue to stay right by their sides and bless them with furniture, housing needs, food, medical and spiritual help. We encourage them during their transitions from being outdoors for years and now adjusting to life indoors with life’s responsibilities. We pick them up and take them to Bible studies, stores, and a once a month family style meal so they can all stay in touch with other. They have become a family
